<objective>
Teach the browser TV runtime to apply selected audio tracks and gate same-file switching by actual capability.

Purpose: TV must not claim a vocal switch succeeded until it can actually select the requested source audio track. If the current browser lacks `audioTracks` or the target track cannot be selected, playback must stay on the committed mode and show a clear Chinese failure notice.
Output: initial playback applies `PlaybackTarget.selectedTrackRef`, `SwitchController` handles `switchKind: "audio_track"` without standby video reload, and unsupported/failure notices stay Chinese-first.
</objective>
