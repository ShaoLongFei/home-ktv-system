<objective>
Resolve real-MV queue playback intent on the server and emit explicit selected track intent to TV.

Purpose: admitted real-MV assets are one `dual-track-video` asset with `vocalMode: "dual"`, so asset metadata alone cannot say whether the TV should play original or accompaniment. Queueing must choose the effective mode from room state and reviewed `trackRoles`, persist that committed mode on the queue entry, and make `PlaybackTarget.selectedTrackRef` non-null for playable real MV.
Output: `add-queue-entry` accepts queueable real-MV assets, stores `playbackOptions.preferredVocalMode`, starts playback with the resolved effective mode, and `buildPlaybackTarget` maps that mode to `trackRoles.original` or `trackRoles.instrumental`.
</objective>
